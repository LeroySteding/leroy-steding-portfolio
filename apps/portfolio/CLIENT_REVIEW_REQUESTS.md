## ‚ú® Tips for Sending Review Requests

### Best Practices

1. **Timing**: 
   - Send 2-4 weeks after project completion
   - Or during project milestones
   - Or after positive feedback moments

2. **Personalization**:
   - Reference specific project details
   - Mention concrete results
   - Acknowledge their busy schedule

3. **Make it Easy**:
   - Provide specific questions
   - Include metrics they can reference
   - Let them reply via email (no forms)
   - Offer to draft something for their approval

4. **Follow-Up**:
   - Wait 1 week before first follow-up
   - Maximum 2 follow-ups
   - Respect "no" gracefully

5. **Incentivize (Optional)**:
   - Offer to write them a recommendation
   - Share their business/website
   - Provide free consultation hour
   - Give them early access to new features

### Email Sending Schedule

**Week 1**: Send initial requests (Monday-Wednesday, 9-11 AM)
- AI ATS client
- 3D CAD Quote Tool client
- SURF team lead

**Week 2**: Follow up on non-responses (Tuesday-Thursday)
- Send gentle reminder

**Week 3**: Send second batch
- Zwempret Amsterdam
- Other Hifive clients
- Former colleagues

**Week 4**: Final follow-ups
- Last gentle nudge for non-responses
